EBI Solutions WordPress site

Main developer

EBI Solutions is a data engineering company of which EBI AI is a part. After demonstrating my web development skills and work ethic to EBI AI, EBI Solutions entrusted me with the task of recreating and transforming their old website into a new WordPress site. I was responsible for building everything from scratch and converting the old website's blocks to new ones, aligning them with the EBI AI website theme.

EBI Solutions is the first website I handled from planning and development through the QA and deployment phases. Therefore, I can confidently say that I am the main and sole developer of this website.
