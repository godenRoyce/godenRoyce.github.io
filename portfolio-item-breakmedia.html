Breakmedia WordPress site

Assistant developer

Breakmedia, https://breakmedia.net/ is the WordPress site of Offshorly’s client, Break. Break is an independent communications agency that delivers digital marketing services to clients in Singapore and Indonesia.

As a junior web developer at Offshorly Ltd, Breakmedia was my first project. Fresh out of college, I primarily shadowed our senior developers and assisted them with their projects. During my first month, I familiarized myself with the templates and codebase we use for our WordPress sites. At Offshorly, we rely on custom templates—our own boilerplate—that includes various website blocks developed from previous projects. When starting a new project, we use this boilerplate as a foundation and create additional blocks as needed based on the design requirements.
