HSP WordPress site

Main developer

The Humanitarian Standards Partnership (HSP) is a network of organizations and initiatives dedicated to establishing standards for quality and accountability in humanitarian action.

The WordPress site for HSP was the first project I independently built and developed. Based on the initial design provided by the client, I created all the web blocks and pages necessary for the site. From the navigation menu to the banners and footer, HSP is the first website I constructed from scratch using our custom WordPress template.

After completing the initial blocks and pages, I handed the project over to a new junior developer. He then continued to refine the website and add new elements according to the client's requests. Despite being a junior developer myself, I took on the responsibility of mentoring and guiding our new junior developer, sharing my knowledge and experience.
